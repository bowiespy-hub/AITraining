Step 2: Translate for Design

Copy facts from NotebookLM into Gemini. Force it into a strict table so Canva can read it.

"Format these 10 facts into a CSV table with exactly two columns: [Hook_Headline] and [Subtext_Data]. CRITICAL RULE: Keep text extremely brief (max 8 words per cell) so it fits onto an Instagram square template."

Step 3: Visual Scale (The Magic)

In Canva, open an IG Post Template with two text boxes.

  • Go to left panel: Apps -> Search for "Bulk Create".
  • Select "Enter Data Manually" and paste the CSV table from Gemini.
  • Right-click text boxes in your template -> "Connect Data" (Link Headline to Headline).
  • Click Generate. You now have 10 beautifully designed carousel slides instantly.
