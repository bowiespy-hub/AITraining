Context

The Raw Material

Provide location, facts, constraints, or the client's current situation before asking anything.

Task

The Action

Be specific: "Summarize this PDF", "Draft a cold email", "Create a comparison table".

WHO

The Pain Point

Identify the target reader (e.g., Foreign Investor vs. First-time Buyer). It is NOT the AI's role.

Tone

The Anchor

Set the vibe: Empathetic, highly analytical, professional, or urgent.

Format

The Boundaries

Set hard limits: Max 150 words, 3 bullet points, or CSV output so it doesn't overflow.

"I am a PropNex consultant specializing in High-Net-Worth Individuals (HNWI) and foreign investments in Kuala Lumpur."

List your banned words to kill the "AI Vibe".

"BANNED WORDS: Luxurious, Unbelievable, Don't miss out, Fantastic. Do not use generic marketing fluff."

3. Output Rules

Set default priorities & formatting habits.

"Always prioritize hard data. Highlight ROI, PSF, and Rental Yield upfront. Never write long essays; use bullet points."

Warning: Know Their Weaknesses

ChatGPT's Weakness

Tends to write robotic, highly structured but generic copy. Needs heavy 'Tone' prompting.

Claude's Weakness

Slightly weaker live web search capabilities compared to others. Relies more on what you upload to it.

Gemini's Weakness

Can sometimes refuse prompts or be overly cautious due to Google's strict safety guardrails.

Perplexity's Weakness

It is a search engine, not a creative writer. Do not ask it to write emotional marketing posts.
