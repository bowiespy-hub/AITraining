Context

The Raw Material

Provide location, facts, constraints, or the client's current situation before asking anything.

Task

The Action

Be specific: "Summarize this PDF", "Draft a cold email", "Create a comparison table".

WHO

The Pain Point

Identify the target reader (e.g., Foreign Investor vs. First-time Buyer). It is NOT the AI's role.

Tone

The Anchor

Set the vibe: Empathetic, highly analytical, professional, or urgent.

Format

The Boundaries

Set hard limits: Max 150 words, 3 bullet points, or CSV output so it doesn't overflow your slides.

Warning: Know Their Weaknesses

ChatGPT's Weakness

Tends to write robotic, highly structured but generic copy. Needs heavy 'Tone' prompting to sound human.

Claude's Weakness

Slightly weaker live web search capabilities compared to others. Relies more on what you upload to it.

Gemini's Weakness

Can sometimes refuse prompts or be overly cautious due to Google's strict safety guardrails.

Perplexity's Weakness

It is a search engine, not a creative writer. Do not ask it to write emotional or creative marketing posts.

Step 1: Ingest & Extract

Upload your Developer Brochure into NotebookLM. We need facts, not fluff.

"Extract the 5 strongest selling points from this document. Output ONLY facts, ROI data, and hard numbers. No marketing adjectives."

Step 2: Format for Design

Copy the facts from NotebookLM into Gemini to structure them for Canva.

"Format these 5 facts into a CSV table with exactly two columns: [Headline] and [Subtext]. Keep text extremely brief, max 8 words per column, suitable for Instagram Carousel slides."

Step 3: Automated Design

In Canva, open an IG Post Template.

  • Go to Apps -> Search for "Bulk Create".
  • Select "Enter Data Manually" and paste the CSV table from Gemini.
  • Right-click text boxes in your template -> "Connect Data" (Link Headline to Headline, Subtext to Subtext).
  • Click "Generate" to instantly produce 5 beautifully designed, factual slides.
